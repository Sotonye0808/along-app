# 5 Tips for Sharing Better Routes

Want to become a trusted route reporter on Along? Follow these tips.

## 1. Be Specific

Include exact locations, landmarks, and stop names. Instead of "Lagos to Ikeja",
try "Mushin to Ikeja via Oshodi".

## 2. Include Fare Information

Fares change frequently. Always include the current fare in at least one step.
This is one of the most valuable pieces of information for other commuters.

## 3. Add Photos

A picture is worth a thousand words. Photos of bus stops, landmarks, and
routes help others verify your information.

## 4. Tag Your Routes

Use relevant tags like "lagos", "oshodi", "keke", or "danfo" to help others
discover your posts.

## 5. Keep It Fresh

Route conditions change. If you take the same route regularly, post updates
when fares change or when there's new construction.
