# Welcome to Along

We're excited to introduce **Along** — a community-powered platform for sharing,
verifying, and discovering transport routes across West Africa.

## Why Along?

For millions of daily commuters in Lagos, Accra, and beyond, getting reliable
transit information is a daily challenge. Which bus route is fastest right now?
Is the fare still ₦200? Is there a traffic diversion on Third Mainland Bridge?

Along solves this by letting the community share real-time route intelligence.

## What You Can Do

- **Share routes** — Post detailed routes with fares, waypoints, and photos
- **Verify information** — Like and comment to help others trust the data
- **Earn rewards** — Build your reputation and earn trust badges
- **Explore** — Discover new routes on the interactive map

## What's Next

We're working on push notifications, enhanced map features, and more. Stay
tuned for updates!
